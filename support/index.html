Frequently asked

Common questions.

How does Macul integrate with my existing EHR?

Placeholder answer. Macul connects via bidirectional sync with major EHR platforms. We handle the mapping, your data stays where it lives, and there's no duplicate data entry required.

Is Macul HIPAA-compliant?

Placeholder answer. Yes — Macul is built HIPAA-aligned from the architecture up, with full audit logging, encryption in transit and at rest, and role-based access control.

How long does onboarding take?

Placeholder answer. A typical practice is fully onboarded in two to four weeks, depending on the complexity of existing integrations.

Who owns the data inside Macul?

Placeholder answer. You do. Your clinical data is yours, portable at any time, and never used for model training without explicit opt-in.

What if something goes wrong during a clinic day?

Placeholder answer. Our support team is reachable by phone, email, and in-app during clinic hours, with escalation paths for urgent issues.
