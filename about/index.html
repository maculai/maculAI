What MaculAI Is

MaculAI is an end-to-end practice platform for ophthalmology that unifies charting, scheduling, billing, communications, and imaging into one system, powered by AI. The work began inside a long established practice on the gulf coast of Florida, the kind of office a community has trusted for generations and the kind of office that has trusted MaculAI to build for it.

At its center is image analysis trained across every modality used in an eye exam, drawing on hundreds of thousands of scans. It is not a replacement for clinical judgment. It is a tool. A second set of eyes that puts the right information in front of the doctor at the right moment, and a fuller picture of every case than any one clinician could hold in memory.

MaculAI is the shift. The future of medicine is taking shape here, in the room where the work happens.

What It Does

Data entry and imaging from every diagnostic machine, all in one place. Clinic turnaround time goes down. More patient-facing time. More personalized care.

Complete patient context at the doctor's fingertips. Imaging, measurements, and changes over time presented together, so the full picture reads in seconds and every decision is made on a more complete view of the case.

Automated referral processing, with patient history that moves with the patient.
