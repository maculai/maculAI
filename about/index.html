About Macul

Built to see

A small team of clinicians, engineers, and designers rethinking how the modern eye practice works.

Our Mission

Clarity, for the people who find it for others.

Ophthalmology is one of the most information-dense specialties in medicine — and one of the most under-served by the software built around it.

We build the infrastructure that lets clinicians focus on the patient in front of them, not the interface between them. Intelligent charting, image-aware diagnostics, and integrations that actually hold — in a single, quiet system.

Our work sits at the intersection of care and computation. Every decision we make is in service of that line.

The Story

A practice-shaped problem.

Macul began in a clinic. Years of watching the same friction — hunting across systems, retyping the same notes, waiting on images — made the shape of the problem obvious. The solution was harder, and that's what we set out to build.

Today, Macul is a platform built by people who know the difference between a tool that looks good in a demo and one that holds up on a Tuesday afternoon.

What we believe

Three principles.

◎

Clarity

Software in a clinic should disappear. We obsess over the moments where attention is most precious — and we make sure we're not one of them.

⊙

Precision

Models are only as trustworthy as the rigor behind them. Everything we ship is validated, auditable, and built to fail gracefully when it must.

◉

Partnership

We work alongside the practices that use us — shaping the product in the room where the work happens, not from a distance.

The Team

Small, deliberate.

Clinicians, ML researchers, and product builders — working closely, shipping carefully.
