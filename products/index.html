THE PLATFORM

The Overview

Most ophthalmology practices today run on a stack of disconnected tools. The EHR does not talk to the imaging machines. The phone system does not talk to the schedule. Billing happens in a separate world from charting. Front desk, scheduling, communications, and clinical documentation all live in different software, with staff acting as the human glue between them.

MaculAI replaces that stack. One platform, eight integrated systems, designed around how an eye practice actually operates. Every doctor's note, every scan, every phone call, every claim, in one system, talking to itself.

The result is a practice that runs on rails. Less time hunting between systems, more time with the patient. Scheduling anomalies caught before they become errors. Clinical changes surfaced before they get missed. The clinic responds to the patient, not to the software.

The Eight Systems
01
Records & Correspondence

Inbound referrals, faxes, and prior records flow into the chart automatically. Outbound referrals carry full relevant history with them. The paperwork moves itself.

02
Patient Communications

An AI phone agent handles routine calls, triages urgency, and structures every interaction into a ticket. Urgent calls escalate to a human in seconds. Routine ones never need to.

03
Smart Scheduling

A rules engine enforces how the practice actually books. Block types, surgery days, no-double-book rules. Cancellations on a doctor's day get rebooked automatically.

04
Diagnostic Integration

Every machine in the lane: OCT, biometry, topography, specular, fundus, visual field. All feeding directly into the chart at the moment of capture. No double entry.

05
Image Intelligence

Trend tracking and change detection across modalities. Macular edema progression, corneal thickness down to the micron, specular cell density changes. The longitudinal picture surfaces when the chart opens.

06
Front Desk

Check-in, insurance verification, intake forms, patient identification. Automated where it can be, structured where it cannot. The first ten minutes of the visit move on rails.

07
Billing & Claims

Coding suggested from chart documentation. Vision and medical claims split correctly. Denials tracked, resubmitted, and followed up without manual chasing.

08
Charting & EHR

The central chart every other system feeds into. Timeline view of the entire patient history readable in 30 seconds. Built around how an ophthalmic exam actually flows.
